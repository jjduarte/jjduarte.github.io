some people have asked us why 'traga as velas' which literally means 'bring out the candles'. well, there's a song written by Zach Gill and sung by Jack Johnson that we simply love! when we had the idea of sharing a cooking blog we didn't know it would be so hard to find a title. at first, we looked for something sweet like 'love and cinnamon' which reminds us of our childhood, but that title isn't that original. anyway, as we're huge fans of Jack Johnson, one of us had an insight inspired by the song Girl I wanna lay you down. well, here it is...

...so turn out the lights, bring out the candles, wrap your arms around my love handles, they say that passion may not always endure but this feeling that i have for you is burning up my world...
